Plain Vanilla Cake Recipe

Description

Moist and Fluffy Vanilla Cake: A timeless recipe for a light and airy cake.

Ingredients

  • Butter (250grms)
  • Sugar (250grms)
  • Eggs (5)
  • Vanilla Flavour (1tsp)
  • Milk (100grms)
  • Flour (250grms)
  • Bakingpowder (1tsp)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven.
  2. Add your butter and sugar to a bowl.
  3. Cream your butter and sugar until its light and fluffy.
  4. Add your beaten eggs and Flavour and mix.
  5. Add flour and milk alternately until all the flour is used and incorporated.
  6. Add in your bakingpowder and mix, do not overmix.
  7. pour in batter into a well greased pan.
  8. Allow to bake in the oven for 25-30 minutes in a preheated oven.
  9. Insert a wooden toothpick in the middle of the cake, if it comes out clean your cake is ready else leave the cake to bake for a few more minutes.
  10. Once it's ready, allow to cool in the plan for five(5) minutes before transfering it to a cooling rack. Don't forget to clear the edges before transferring to the cooling rack.
  11. And your cake is ready, enjoy plain or frost with any frosting of choice.
