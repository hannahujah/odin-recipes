Pillow Doughnut Recipe

Description

Fluffy Pillow Doughnuts: Soft, Pillowy and Delicious

Ingredients

  • Flour (250grms)
  • Bakingpowder (1tsp)
  • Sugar (1/4)
  • Yeast (1tbsp)
  • 1 Large egg
  • 1/4 cup warm milk
  • Unsalted melted butter (2tbsp)
  • vanilla extract (1tsp)
  • Oil for frying

Steps

  1. Combine Dry Ingredients: In a medium bowl, Whisk together the flour, bakingpowder, yeast, and sugar.
  2. Combine Wet Ingredients: In a seperate bowl, whisk together the egg, milk, melted butter, and vanilla extract
  3. Mix Wet and Dry: Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients, mixing until just combined. Be careful not to overmix.
  4. Transfer Dough to a Well floured Surface: Beat dough on a well floured surface until soft and stretchy.
  5. Weigh dough and Roll: Weigh 80grms of dough each and roll on surface until round and smooth.
  6. Allow dough to rise: cover dough with kitchen towel and place in the oven or a warm place and allow to rise for 40-45 minutes.
  7. frying: after dough has risen for 40-45 minutes carefully place in a medium heat oil and allow both sides to fry for 3-5 minutes on a low heat.
  8. After frying allow doughnuts to cool and eat plain or coated, filled with any filling of choice and enjoy.
