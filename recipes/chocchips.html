Chocolate Chips Cookies

Description

Classic Chocolate Chips Cookies: A timeless soft and chewy treat

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Flavour
  • Flour
  • Bakingpowder
  • Chocolate Chips

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven.
  2. Add butter and sugar in a bowl.
  3. Cream butter and sugar until it's light and fluffy.
  4. Add in eggs and flavour and mix.
  5. Add in flour and bakingpowder and mix thoroughly.
  6. Lastly add in your chocolate chips and mix.
  7. Scoop batter onto your baking tray lined with parchment paper.
  8. Bake in the oven for 10-12 minutes.
  9. Allow cookies to cool on the baking tray so it can be fully done.
